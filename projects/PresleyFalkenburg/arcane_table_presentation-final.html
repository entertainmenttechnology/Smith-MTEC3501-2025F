The Gap We're Filling
Physical vs Digital Divide
Love collecting rare cards but want dynamic gameplay. Miss the social aspect of face-to-face gaming in digital games.
Static Card Limitations
Physical cards can't evolve, gain experience, or show battle damage. Limited to printed stats and artwork.
Accessibility of Magic
Expensive hologram technology vs accessible projection mapping. Every kitchen table becomes a dueling arena.

Technical Milestones
Hardware Setup
Assemble overhead camera and projector rig. Implement calibration system for alignment. Test detection accuracy in controlled environment.
Card Recognition
Build OpenCV pipeline for detecting standard playing cards or existing TCG cards. Reliable recognition with template matching and contour detection algorithms.
Basic Game Logic
Turn-based combat with health/attack stats. Card placement triggers, win/lose conditions, simple animation system.
System Integration
Connect vision system to game engine. Real-time projection of card stats and battle effects. End-to-end gameplay testing.
